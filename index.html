Roller Bench

Roller Bench, Brian Rush, 2011, powder-coated aluminum and steel rollers, 36" x 78.5" x 28.5"

Description

Bringing people together since 2011, the Roller Bench is guaranteed to make your customers smile. Originally titled simply "Bench" by artist Brian Rush, its users have been making it their own with nicknames like The Friendship Bench and The Can't-Stay-Mad-At-You Bench
