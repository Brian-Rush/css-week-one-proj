Roller Bench

Description

Bringing people together since 2011, the Roller Bench is guaranteed to make people smile. Originally titled simply "Bench" by artist Brian Rush, its users have been making it their own with nicknames like The Friendship Bench and The Can't-Stay-Mad-At-You Bench.
