About the Artist

Brian Rush wants to make you embarrass yourself. But only a little bit. Just as a bit of confusion always precedes learning, embarrassment is the harbinger of social growth.

Brian Rush works as a UX designer and artist in Portland, OR. He loves spoonerisms and sheyboard kortcuts.
